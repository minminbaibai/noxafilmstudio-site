Submit Your Screenplay

Share your vision with our innovative studio.

NOXA FILM is constantly seeking bold, original screenplays that push the boundaries of visual storytelling. We're particularly interested in narratives that can be enhanced by our unique AI-assisted production approach. Whether you have a completed feature, a promising short, or even just a treatment with strong visual potential, we invite you to submit your work for consideration.

Submission Guidelines

  • Scripts must be submitted in PDF format only
  • Please ensure your script follows industry standard formatting
  • We accept both WGA and non-WGA registered scripts
  • Response time varies, but we aim to review all submissions within 4-6 weeks
  • Due to volume, we cannot provide detailed feedback on all submissions
  • By submitting, you confirm this is your original work and you have the rights to submit it

Note: This form will open your email client with the form information. Please attach your script PDF to the email before sending.
